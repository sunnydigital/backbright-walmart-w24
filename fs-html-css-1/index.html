I have an amazing guacamole recipe to share with you!

What you'll need:

  • 1x Avacado 🥑
  • Peppercorn 🌶️
  • Salt 🧂
  • Onions (crunchy, springy) 🧅
  1. Scoop out the Avacado 🥑 and place contents into a bowl
  2. Using a spoon, mix together thoroughly the contents
  3. Add 7 spins of peppercorn
  4. Add 5 shakes of salt
  5. Add 2 chunked slices of onion
  6. Et, voila! You have your springy guacamole!
